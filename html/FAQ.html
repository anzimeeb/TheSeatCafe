Frequently Asked Question’s

Curious about The Seat Cafe? We've compiled answers to some of your most frequently asked questions. For more information, feel free to reach out!

Our coffee shop is open from Monday—Thursday: 11am-10pm, Friday—Sunday: 10am-10:30pm.

Yes, we currently have a delivery partnership with Food Panda.

All our cakes and pastries are freshly baked in-house every day using high-quality ingredients.

Unfortunately, we don't offer catering services. However, you can place large orders by contacting us directly, and we'll be happy to assist with your needs.

Yes, we have a variety of teas, matchas, and fresh juices available. We also offer a selection of soft drinks.

Yes, we offer free Wi-Fi for all our customers. Just ask our staff for the password.

We do not take reservations for regular seating, but we do have a community table that can be reserved for small group gatherings. Please contact us to check availability.

We are following all local health guidelines, which include regular sanitation, social distancing, and contactless payment options. Our staff members wear masks, and we encourage customers to do the same.

While we can't customize orders, you can add additional items to your order. Please let us know what you need when placing your order, and we'll do our best to accommodate.

Yes, we have a parking lot adjacent to the shop with ample parking space for our customers.

Yes, we are a pet-friendly coffee shop! Well-behaved dogs are welcome in our outdoor seating area. We even have water bowls and treats available for your furry friends.

We value your feedback! You can provide feedback directly on our website, through our social media channels, or by speaking with a manager in-store.

Yes, we are always looking for enthusiastic individuals to join our team. Please visit our Careers page on our website for current job openings and application instructions.

Yes, we offer a loyalty program where you can earn points with each purchase that can be redeemed for discounts and free items. Additionally, we occasionally have special promotions and discounts like student discounts, so be sure to follow us on social media.

We accept all major credit and debit cards, as well as Gcash. Cash payments are also accepted.

Yes, our coffee shop is fully accessible, with ramps and accessible seating areas to accommodate all our customers comfortably.

Absolutely! We encourage customers to bring their own reusable cups and offer a small discount as a thank-you for helping us reduce waste.

Yes, we have sugar-free syrups available for our coffee drinks. Just let us know your preference when placing your order.

Yes, we have outdoor seating. You can enjoy the breeze outside while sipping coffee.

Yes, we accept special orders for cakes and pastries for birthdays, anniversaries, and other special occasions. Please contact us in advance to discuss your requirements and place your order.
