Celebrate Independence Day at The Seat Café + Pantry!

Independence Day is just around the corner, and The Seat Café + Pantry is ready to help you relax and enjoy this special holiday. Join us for a day of refreshing drinks, live music, and community fun. Here’s what we have in store for you this Independence Day:

Special Independence Day Promo
Celebrate the spirit of freedom with our exclusive Independence Day offer! Purchase a 12oz Iced Kiwi, Iced Macadamia Latte, or Iced Blueberry, and get a second drink for free. This promotion is available only on Independence Day, so don’t miss out on this refreshing deal.

Share Your Moments
We love seeing our community come together to celebrate. Share your Independence Day moments at The Seat Café + Pantry on social media with the hashtag #IndependenceDayAtTheSeat. Tag us in your posts for a chance to be featured on our page!

Join Us for a Day of Relaxation and Fun!
Take a break from the hustle and bustle and enjoy a relaxing day at The Seat Café + Pantry this Independence Day. Whether you’re stopping by for breakfast, lunch, or an evening coffee, we’ve got everything you need to make your day special.
