MOM • A Mother's Day Celebration

Celebrate Mother's Day with us at The Seat! We have a special treat planned to honor the incredible moms who bring so much love and joy into our lives.

Special Gifts for Mom 1. Flower Stems for All Mothers On May 12th, every mother who visits The Seat will receive a beautiful stem of flowers, a small token of our appreciation for all that they do. It's our way of adding a touch of beauty to their special day.

2. Complimentary Polaroid Pictures Capture the moment with a free Polaroid picture! Simply ask any of our baristas or staff, and they'll be delighted to assist you in creating a lovely keepsake. These memories are on us, at no cost to you.

Event Details
• Date: Sunday, May 12th, 2024
• Time: Open from 10am to midnight
Join us at The Seat to celebrate and honor the wonderful mothers in our lives. We look forward to sharing this special day with you!

Share Your Moments
We love seeing your happy moments at The Seat! Share your Mother's Day celebrations on social media using the hashtag #theseatcafe. Whether it’s a snapshot of your flower, your Polaroid picture, or simply enjoying the day with your loved ones, we can’t wait to see your posts!

Giving Back
This Mother's Day, we’re also giving back to our community. A portion of our sales on May 12th will be donated to [Local Women's Shelter/Charity], supporting mothers and families in need. Join us in making a positive impact.
