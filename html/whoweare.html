About Us

The Seat Café + Pantry is more than just a café; it's an extension of Zarate Manila's creative vision and design excellence. Founded in 2022, The Seat showcases the unique furniture designs of Zarate Manila's owner, reflecting his meticulous attention to detail and passion for craftsmanship.

We transform our interior and theme annually to keep things fresh and exciting. Each year, new furniture pieces, fresh color palettes, and innovative décor create a dynamic environment that inspires creativity and comfort, ensuring our patrons always have something new to look forward to.

A Hub For Connection

At The Seat Café + Pantry, we believe in the power of community and connections. Our café is a vibrant gathering spot where people come together to share ideas, collaborate, and build meaningful relationships. Filled with unique furniture crafted by Zarate Manila, our cozy and creatively designed space is perfect for casual conversations, productive meetings, or quiet moments. Whether you're catching up with friends or networking with colleagues, The Seat Café + Pantry is your go-to destination for connection and community.

Come Join Us

Join us at The Seat Café + Pantry for an unforgettable experience with delicious food, refreshing drinks, and a welcoming atmosphere. Whether you're looking for a cozy corner to unwind, meeting friends, or needing a vibrant space to work, we have something for everyone.

We're also looking for talented individuals to join our team. If you're a local artisan, musician, or dedicated worker, we welcome you with open arms. Together, let's create magic and make The Seat Café + Pantry a place where dreams come true.
