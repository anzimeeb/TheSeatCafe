Embrace the Paw-sitive Vibes: Welcome to Our Pet-Friendly Cafe at The Seat Café + Pantry!

At The Seat Café + Pantry, we understand that pets are part of the family. That's why we're excited to announce that our cafe is now officially pet-friendly! We believe that every coffee break should be enjoyed with your furry companions by your side. Here's what you need to know about our pet-friendly environment:

Bring Your Furry Friends, Strays Included!
Whether you have a playful pup, a cuddly cat, or a stray looking for a loving home, they're all welcome at The Seat Café + Pantry. Feel free to bring your furry friends along for a coffee date or a leisurely stroll. Our spacious seating area and outdoor patio provide plenty of room for your pets to relax and mingle while you savor your favorite brew.

Treats for Pets and Owners
At The Seat Café + Pantry, we love spoiling both our human and furry customers. While we don't have a menu specifically for pets, we're happy to provide water and treats for all our furry visitors. Treat your furry companion to a tasty biscuit or a refreshing bowl of water while you enjoy your coffee break together.

Share Your Pet Pics
We love seeing our furry visitors enjoying their time at The Seat Café + Pantry. Share your adorable pet pics on social media using the hashtag #PetFriendlyAtTheSeat. Tag us in your posts for a chance to be featured on our page!

Join Us for Pet-Friendly Events
Keep an eye out for special pet-friendly events at The Seat Café + Pantry. From puppy playdates to pet costume contests, we're always planning fun activities for you, your pets, and our community strays to enjoy together.
