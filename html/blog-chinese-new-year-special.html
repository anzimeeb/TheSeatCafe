February 1, 2022

Welcome the Year of Tiger with Us!

Ring in the Year of the Tiger with us at The Seat Café + Pantry! We’re excited to celebrate Chinese New Year with a range of special events, delicious treats, and festive activities. Here’s how we’re bringing the joy and traditions of the Lunar New Year to our café:

Free Tikoy for the First 100 Customers
To mark the occasion, we’re offering a special treat for our loyal customers. The first 100 customers with a minimum purchase of P588 in a single receipt will receive a free box of Tikoy (Nian Gao). This delicious, traditional Chinese New Year cake symbolizes good luck and prosperity, and is a wonderful way to start the new year. This promotion is valid only on February 1, 2022, so be sure to come early!

Share Your New Year Wishes
Join our community wall where you can write and share your New Year wishes. Whether it’s a hope for health, happiness, or success, share your dreams and let’s start the new year with positive energy and good vibes.
